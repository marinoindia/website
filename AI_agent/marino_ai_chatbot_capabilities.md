# AI Chatbot Capabilities for Marino Corporation of India Website

Website: https://marinoindia.co.in/

## Purpose of the AI Chatbot

The AI chatbot will function as a **digital sales and support
assistant** for website visitors. Its primary goals are:

-   Guide visitors to the correct products
-   Capture sales inquiries and leads
-   Answer common product and company questions
-   Provide quick contact options (WhatsApp, phone, email)
-   Improve conversion of website visitors into customers

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Capabilities

## 1. Product Discovery Assistant

The chatbot should help visitors quickly find the right product.

Example flow:

User: "I need a wire rope sling"

Bot: - What capacity do you need? - 1 Ton - 5 Ton - 10 Ton - Custom
Capacity

Bot then directs the user to the relevant product page on the website.

Supported product categories:

-   Steel Wire Rope
-   Wire Rope Slings
-   Industrial Chains
-   Lifting Hooks
-   Thimbles for Wire Ropes
-   Chain Pulley Blocks
-   Lifting Equipment

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Lead Generation & Inquiry Capture

The chatbot should collect visitor details when they request a quotation
or product information.

Information to collect:

-   Name
-   Company Name
-   Product Requirement
-   Quantity
-   Phone Number
-   Email Address

Collected inquiries should be sent to:

-   Company Email
-   WhatsApp
-   CRM or lead system

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Instant WhatsApp Connection

The chatbot should allow visitors to directly contact the company via
WhatsApp.

Example:

"Would you like to speak with our team on WhatsApp?"

Click button → Opens WhatsApp chat with company number.

Recommended WhatsApp number: +91 98311 44669

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ Support)

The chatbot should automatically answer common questions such as:

### Products

-   Do you supply steel wire ropes?
-   What sizes are available?
-   Do you provide custom lifting slings?
-   What load capacities are available?

### Business Information

-   Company location
-   Office address
-   Business hours
-   Contact numbers
-   Email address

### Orders & Supply

-   Minimum order quantity
-   Delivery timelines
-   Custom manufacturing availability

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Product Specification Assistance

The chatbot should guide users through technical product selection.

Example:

User: "I need lifting chain"

Bot asks:

-   What capacity?
-   What length?
-   What application?

Then recommends appropriate product pages.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Catalog and Document Sharing

The chatbot should allow users to download:

-   Product catalog
-   Technical specification sheets
-   Company profile
-   Certifications

Example:

"Download our lifting equipment catalog here."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 7. Government & Tender Support

Since many industrial buyers are government or institutional buyers, the
chatbot should answer questions like:

-   Do you supply to government organizations?
-   Are you registered on GeM?
-   Do you provide BIS compliant products?
-   Can you participate in tenders?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 8. Website Navigation Assistance

The chatbot should guide visitors to important pages:

-   Products
-   About Us
-   Contact Page
-   Inquiry Form
-   Certifications

Example:

User: "Where are you located?"

Bot: "Our office is located in Kidderpore, Kolkata. Would you like
directions or contact details?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 9. Escalation to Human Support

If the chatbot cannot answer a question, it should escalate the request.

Example:

"I will connect you with our team for accurate information."

Options:

-   WhatsApp
-   Phone call
-   Email inquiry form

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Recommended Chatbot Behavior

The chatbot should:

-   Be professional and concise
-   Focus on product assistance
-   Prioritize lead capture
-   Encourage direct contact with the company

The chatbot should NOT:

-   Provide final pricing automatically
-   Provide engineering calculations
-   Give commitments on delivery or specifications without human
    confirmation

------------------------------------------------------------------------
